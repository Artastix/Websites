Who are we?

.Hacktr is a community dedicated to bringing coders together to create great things. We organize hackatrons, programming seminars and events designed to introduce new blood to the world of coding.

Our annual assemblies are some of the largest in the United States, but we also stretch farther with entities in Europe.

  • Hackatron

    We organize this event in collaboration with well-known brands such as Microsoft, Apple, Google. etc. It is our largest convention and the kickstart of our brand.

  • DevTalks

    An exciting event in which we gather renowned speakers, industry leaders and aspiring developers to participate and discuss the trends and future of development.

  • Web4U

    We organize periodic seminars for web development and IT engineering with a focus on both back-end and front-end. These classes are conducted by trained professionals and industry leaders.

  • iTeens

    A week-long event dedicated to introducing children and teenagers to the world of technology and explore the possibility of pursuing an education in web development, robotics and 3D architecture.
